Science & Art

Science and art are often viewed as opposing disciplines, yet they are truly the two inseparable sides of the same coin of human creativity. While they employ different methodologies, both are driven by an innate desire to explore the unknown and give form to the formless.

Science searches for truth outside, looking toward the physical universe to decode the laws of nature through observation, logic, and empirical evidence. In contrast, art searches for truth inside, diving into the depths of the human psyche to express the subjective realities of emotion, intuition, and experience. Together, they form a complete picture of our existence.
